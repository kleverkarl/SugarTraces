SugarTraces Survey

Purpose of Our Research

We are conducting this survey in order for you, the participant, to aide our endeavors in creating a smoother and more effective experience for SugarTraces. In this study, we are mainly focusing on the persuasiveness of the different feedback messages within the application and its effects on the user on both a personal level and a habitual context. By participating in the survey, you agree for the analysis of the results in this survey to be used for further development of the application.

No personally identifiable information will be collected in our survey. We respect your trust and protect your privacy, we will only use this data solely for research purposes.

If you have any questions, please e-mail wsl-research@dcs.upd.edu.ph
