SugarTraces

What is SugarTraces?

SugarTraces is a mobile health application, which aims to help users with prediabetes and diabetes to live a healthier lifestyle by maintaining healthy blood glucose levels. The feedback system is placed to respond to glucose readings that makes this application interactive and entertaining. Apart from this, updates will be provided to the user based on the readings it receives which unlocks achievements. This serves as a reward system that incentivizes consistent use, thus becoming an effective tool for behavior change for the user.

Features

Logging Data

Data regarding your blood glucose is saved whenever you log your information in.

Logging will only be stored locally and the data will never be sent to us.

Statistics

The data and information that is logged is easily and readily available for viewing purposes using graphs and statistics, based on the logged data through time.

Profile Page

Your personal data can be easily viewed, along with the number of days that normal blood glucose levels have been logged in a row.

Achievements

While using the application, achievements may be unlocked. Can you unlock them all?

Privacy Policy

SugarTraces is a mobile health application developed by the Web Science Lab of the Department of Science, College of Engineering, University of the Philippines - Diliman. By using SugarTraces, you consent to the terms and conditions described in this Privacy Policy.

SugarTraces Does Not Collect Any Information

SugarTraces will request permissions to read information such as date of birth and sex. Additionally, it will also request permissions to read and write blood glucose readings. Collection of name, date of birth and sex is only used for customization and they are only stored on the device and will not be stored in a cloud.

The user has the ability to export their data to us through e-mail. The exportable data will only contain the user's sex, blood glucose readings as well as the dates the readings were recorded. The exportable data will not include any personally identifiable information.
