SugarTraces Research

Purpose of Our Research

The Web Science Laboratory of the Department of Computer Science of the University of the Philippines - Diliman is doing research on the impact of technology in healthcare. One of our research projects is SugarTraces, a mobile application that helps users manage their diabetes by providing a tool to record their blood sugar level readings. In this particular research study, we are trying determine the effects of using persuasive technologies in influencing the behavior of users in managing diabetes.

Who can participate?

Anyone who has used our SugarTraces mobile application, preferably multiple times, is encouraged to participate in our study.

How to participate?

Download the SugarTraces mobile application on iOS App Store or Google Play Store. After you have used it several times, please answer the questionnaire.

Privacy Policy
