SugarTraces Research

Purpose of Our Research

We, the team behind SugarTraces, are committed towards being able to provide an application that helps users become aware of their own blood glucose levels. We are conducting this study in order for you, the participant, to aide our endeavors in creating a smoother and more effective experience for SugarTraces. We place our focus on the persuasiveness of the different feedback messages within the application and its effects on the user on both a personal level and a habitual context. Through this persuasive technology, we hope to be able to attain consistency towards blood glucose measurement and living a healthier lifestyle for our users.

Anyone is able to join this study as long as the individual has a smartphone that supports either iOS or Android with a copy of SugarTraces (available on the App Store and Google Play Store) on the phone.

By participating in the study, you agree for the analysis of the results in this study to be used for further development of the application.

No personally identifiable information will be collected in our study. We respect your trust and protect your privacy, we will only use this data solely for research purposes.
